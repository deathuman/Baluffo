Data sources

This page prioritizes verifiable game-development listings from specialized boards, major job platforms, and ATS-hosted career pages:

  • Community Curated Google Sheet - Crowdsourced game dev job listings from the community
  • LinkedIn Jobs - Major global source for studio and publisher roles
  • Indeed - High-volume aggregator with broad country coverage
  • Hitmarker - Large gaming and esports-focused job platform
  • Work With Indies - Curated indie game jobs and hiring community
  • InGame Job - Dedicated games-industry board across multiple regions
  • Games Jobs Direct - Long-running games recruitment marketplace
  • GamesIndustry.biz Jobs - Industry publication job board
  • Wellfound - Useful for startup and early-stage game studio hiring
  • Greenhouse Job Boards - Direct company ATS listings used by many studios
  • Lever Jobs - Direct company ATS listings used by game teams
  • Workday Careers - Enterprise ATS used by major publishers and large studios

Social channels (for example X/Twitter hashtags, forum threads, and subreddit posts) are useful for discovery, but are not treated as primary reliable sources for live listings.
